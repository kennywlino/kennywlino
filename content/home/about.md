# Biography
Hello there! I am a second year master's student in the [Erasmus Mundus Language and Communication Technologies](https://lct-master.org) program. I am most interested in combining technology, education and natural language processing to enhance second language learners’abilities to learn languages, particularly in the domain of speech. I am also curious about language variation within natural language data and using this information to better understand populations.
